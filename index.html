项目简介

Urban public spaces such as streets, parks, and campuses face persistent challenges in maintaining cleanliness due to scattered waste including plastic bottles, cigarette butts, leaves, broken glass, and other debris.

Public area cleanliness is a growing concern in urban environments. Traditional manual cleaning methods are labor-intensive, inefficient, and expose workers to hazards such as broken glass and sharp objects.

This project aims to design and prototype an autonomous robot that combines targeted grasping with broad-area sweeping and compression. The system will use computer vision and lidar to navigate and operate in public spaces.
